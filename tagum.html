TAGUM CITY

The Music Capital of the South

Tagum City, the capital of Davao del Norte, is a vibrant urban center celebrated as the "Music Capital of the South" and the "City of Festivals." It serves as a major economic and cultural crossroads in Mindanao, known for its well-organized layout and iconic palm-lined avenues like Daang Maharlika. The city seamlessly blends modern progress with a clean, hospitable atmosphere, featuring architectural landmarks like the New City Hall alongside serene green spaces like the San Agustin Botanical Park. Whether for its lively cultural celebrations or its status as a hub for commerce and the arts, Tagum stands as a premier destination in the region.

TOP PLACES IN TAGUM

New City Hall

A striking architectural icon of Tagum City, known for its eco-friendly design and impressive modern structure that welcomes visitors and locals alike.

San Agustin Botanical Park

A refreshing green sanctuary filled with lush gardens, perfect for relaxation, nature walks, and peaceful escapes.

Banana Beach Resort

A tropical getaway offering relaxing seaside views and a cozy atmosphere, ideal for family bonding and weekend retreats.

Christ the King Cathedral

A majestic spiritual landmark featuring elegant architecture and a serene atmosphere for reflection and worship.
