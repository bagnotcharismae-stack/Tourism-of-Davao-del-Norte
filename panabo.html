PANABO CITY

The Banana Capitol of the Philippines

Panabo City, famously known as the "Banana Capital of the Philippines," is a fast-growing coastal city that serves as a vital economic and agricultural hub in Davao del Norte. Strategically located between Tagum and Davao City, it is home to vast, world-class banana plantations and the modern Panabo Wharf, which facilitates the international export of the region's premium produce. Beyond its industrial strength, the city offers peaceful recreational spots like the Freedom Park and Agri-Tourism sites, providing a unique blend of a bustling commercial environment and a serene, green countryside atmosphere.

TOP PLACES IN PANABO

Mangrove Forest Park

A serene eco-park featuring lush mangrove forests, offering a peaceful escape and a chance to connect with nature.

The Panabo Park

A vibrant green space where locals gather for leisure, outdoor activities, and community events.

Panabo City Hall

A modern civic landmark representing the city’s growth, featuring a clean and welcoming environment.
