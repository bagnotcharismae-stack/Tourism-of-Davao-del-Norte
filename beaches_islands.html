BEACHES & ISLANDS

Pristine Coasts and Crystal-Clear Waters

Davao del Norte’s coastline is defined by the pristine beauty of the Island Garden City of Samal, a world-class destination renowned for its powdery white-sand beaches, secluded coves, and luxury waterfront resorts. Beyond the main island, the province offers hidden gems like Talicud Island, where crystal-clear waters and vibrant coral reefs provide a sanctuary for divers and snorkelers, and the enchanting Vanishing Island, a sandbar that emerges and retreats with the tides. From the protected giant clam sanctuaries in the Davao Gulf to the unique blend of forest and sea at Banana Beach, the region’s islands and coastal stretches serve as a breathtaking showcase of Mindanao’s rich marine biodiversity and tropical serenity.

TOP BEACHES & ISLANDS IN DAVAO DEL NORTE

Samal Island (Island Garden City of Samal)

Famous for white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and resorts, ideal for swimming and snorkeling.

Pearl Farm Beach

Known for luxury resorts, powdery sand, and water sports activities.

Talikud Island

Offers pristine beaches and diving spots, popular for snorkeling and marine life exploration.
