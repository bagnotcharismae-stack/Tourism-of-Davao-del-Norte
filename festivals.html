FESTIVALS

Celebrating the Rich Culture of Davao del Norte

As a celebrated "Land of Festivals," the province hosts a year-round calendar of grand events that honor everything from artistic excellence to agricultural abundance. Major highlights include the Musikahan Festival in Tagum, which celebrates the region's musical prowess, and the Caracoles Festival on Samal Island, a colorful tribute to marine biodiversity. These celebrations unite the community through street dancing, traditional music, and agri-trade fairs, reflecting a spirit of thanksgiving and the diverse heritage of the Filipino people.

TOP FESTIVALS IN DAVAO DEL NORTE

Sikwate Festival

A celebration highlighting local cocoa products and the tradition of making sikwate, a native hot chocolate drink.

Banana Festival

Showcases the significance of banana farming through competitions, exhibits, and community festivities.

Ata Manobo Festival

Dedicated to the indigenous Ata Manobo people, showcasing their dances, rituals, and colorful traditions.
