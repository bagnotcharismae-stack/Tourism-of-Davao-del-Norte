NATURE

Explore the Green Spaces and Natural Wonders

Davao del Norte is a sanctuary of diverse landscapes, ranging from the turquoise coastal waters of the Gulf to the dense, mist-covered mountain ranges of the highlands. The province is home to unique ecological wonders, such as the world-record-holding Monfort Bat Sanctuary and the enchanting underground river systems and caves of Kapalong. Nature lovers can explore a variety of environments, from the refreshing multi-tiered Hagimit Falls to the sprawling, symmetrical palm-lined avenues and botanical parks that define its urban centers. Whether trekking through the "forest frontiers" of the north or relaxing on the white sandbars that appear and vanish with the tides, the province offers an immersive experience into the rich biodiversity and preserved natural beauty of Mindanao.
