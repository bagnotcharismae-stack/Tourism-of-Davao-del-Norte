SAMAL CITY

Island Garden Paradise of the South

Samal City, officially known as the Island Garden City of Samal, is a premier tropical destination in Davao del Norte famous for its pristine white-sand beaches and crystal-clear turquoise waters. As the largest resort city in the Philippines, it offers a diverse mix of natural wonders, ranging from the refreshing Hagimit Falls and the world-renowned Monfort Bat Sanctuary to the vibrant marine life surrounding Talicud Island. Whether you are looking for high-end coastal relaxation, adventurous snorkeling, or a peaceful escape into lush island greenery, Samal provides a perfect sanctuary that balances ecological beauty with warm Filipino hospitality.

TOP PLACES IN SAMAL

Samal Island

A tropical paradise with pristine beaches, turquoise waters, and a relaxing island atmosphere.

Talikud Island

A hidden paradise known for its white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant marine life, perfect for snorkeling and peaceful island escapes.

Monfort Bat Sanctuary

Home to millions of bats, this world-famous sanctuary offers a unique wildlife and ecological experience.

Vanishing Island

A unique sandbar that appears during low tide, offering a magical experience in the middle of the sea.
