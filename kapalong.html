KAPALONG

Home of Caves and Adventure

Kapalong is widely recognized as the "Caving Capital of Mindanao," offering a thrilling destination for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts. The municipality is famous for its extensive network of limestone caves and underground wonders, such as the Suaon Nature Park, which features stunning stalactites, stalagmites, and crystal-clear subterranean rivers. Beyond its geological marvels, Kapalong is a major agricultural hub with vast plantations and lush forests, providing a unique blend of extreme outdoor exploration and serene, green landscapes.

TOP PLACES IN KAPALONG

Tagusab Falls

A scenic waterfall perfect for nature lovers.

Kapalong River

A peaceful river ideal for relaxation.

Mainit Sulfuric Spring

A natural hot spring with therapeutic benefits.
