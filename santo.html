SANTO TOMAS

Gateway of Growth and Agriculture

Santo Tomas is a progressive municipality in Davao del Norte, widely recognized as a major agricultural powerhouse and a leader in the region’s banana industry. Often referred to as a "Gateway of Growth," the town features a vast landscape of world-class plantations that support both the local economy and international exports. Beyond its industrial significance, Santo Tomas offers a blend of rural charm and modern development, with community landmarks like the Municipal Plaza and natural escapes like the Tibal-og River reflecting its peaceful yet fast-evolving character.
